Library Management System

The purpose of a library management system is to operate a library with efficiency and at reduced costs. The system being entirely automated streamlines all the tasks involved in operations of the library. The activities of cataloging, indexing, issuing, returning, stock checking are done by the software. Such software eliminates the need for repetitive manual work and minimizes the chances of errors.

Managing a library manually is labor intensive and an immense amount of paperwork is involved. An automated system reduces the need for manpower and stationery. This leads to lower operational costs. The system saves time for both the user and the librarian. With just a click the user can search for the books available in the library. The librarian can answer queries with ease regarding the availability of books. Adding, removing or editing the database is a simple process. Adding new members or cancelling existing memberships can be done with ease.

Usecases of Library Management System

1) Student Registeration:

Inorder to use Library Management System, Student will be required to register him/herself. Students can register through online by entering name, email id, Registration number and password. Registeration of students will allow the system to keep the record of student activities. Record keeping of student activities will help the admin organize and manage data that will be visible to the student in the dashboard. Student Registeration will help the admin determine which student is needed to return the book or which student the book should be issued.

Student Registeration will enable the students to use the features of library Management System. They will have their dashboard from where they can all the details regarding book catalog, issued books and books to be returned at a certain date. After the registeration students will be a given a special registration token which will allow them to select the books and borrow them. This registeration token will stay with the student until he cancels his registeration.

2) Book Catalog:

After Registeration students will be able to view the library. All the books will be available in the library categorized into different categories. Books might be categorized into different genres or they might be categorized alphabets. Although if a student is failed to find his book he can search through the catalog by name of the book. The search feature will be available with different filters to apply. Students will be able to search the books with respect to the year of publish, by the author name or by the rating of the book.

3) Issue Book:

After the book selection, Student can request the admin to borrow the book for a limited time period. Inorder to borrow, he must be registered in the system and have his registeration token with him. The process of borrowing will involve book_id, Borrower_Name, Reg_token, Issuer_Name, Date_of_Issue and date_of_return. All the information will be stored in the database. After succesfull issue of the book, the user will be sent a notification of confirmation with a date to return the book. The issued book will be available to see in the dashboard under the issued book section for the student.
